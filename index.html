The Author

Wild Canopy is a space where words grow wild, and stories breathe — a place where emotion meets reflection, and imagination finds its home. Here, I explore the human experience in all its depth: love, longing, vulnerability, and the quiet strength that carries us forward.

I am a writer and a law student, navigating the intersection of reason and feeling. My legal studies sharpen my understanding of justice, morality, and human complexity, while my writing gives voice to the hearts and stories that law alone cannot capture.

From Almost, Then Always to Fallen, All Because of Him, The Loop, and Wolfire, each story is a journey — a reflection on choices, consequences, and the enduring power of resilience. Through prose that is both deliberate and lyrical, I aim to illuminate the spaces where hearts struggle, grow, and heal.

Wild Canopy is for readers who seek depth, beauty, and truth in storytelling — a canopy under which every emotion is honored, every story cherished.

Welcome. Explore. Let the stories linger.

The Collections

Almost, Then Always

Elena never meant to fall in love over a chessboard.

It was supposed to be a simple friendship—quiet conversations, shared laughter, and games that ended in gentle checkmates. But somewhere between late replies, unspoken fears, and the slow distance that crept between them, love became something heavier than either of them expected.

As Elena struggles to hold on and Adrian struggles to understand what feels, their relationship drifts between closeness and silence, hope and almosts. What begins as a chance meeting becomes a story of timing, vulnerability, and the terrifying honesty that love demands.

Almost, Then Always is a reflective, deeply emotional journey about loving first, losing slowly, and discovering that sometimes the hardest part isn't letting go—it's learning how to stay.
